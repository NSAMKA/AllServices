Frequently Asked Questions

These FAQs aim to provide clarity and guidance on using "All Service." If you have more specific questions, feel free to reach out to our support team or refer to our comprehensive user guide.

1. What is "All Service" and how does it work?

All Services is an app designed to connect users with various service providers for tasks ranging from cleaning and plumbing to pet care and more. Simply download the app, browse services, and connect with skilled professionals.

2. How do I sign up as a service provider?

Service providers can register on the app by submitting necessary details through the "JOIN US" section. Our team will review the application, ensuring the quality and reliability of our service network.

3. How can I pay for services on the app?

We offer multiple secure payment options, including credit/debit cards and digital wallets. Choose the payment method that suits you best.

4. What measures are taken to ensure the safety of users and service providers?

All service providers undergo a thorough background check before joining the platform. User reviews and ratings further contribute to a trustworthy community. In case of concerns, our support team is available.

5. Can I track the arrival of a service provider in real-time?

Yes, once a service is booked, you can track the location and estimated arrival time of your service provider in real-time through the app.
